Adults in Washington DC used the most Marijuana in 2019-2020.

The District of Columbia, has the largest adult population of marijuana users in the United States. Out of all states sampled for the recreational and medicinal use of Marijuana between 2019 and 2020, 135,000 adults indicated that they used Marijuana at least once during the year. This constitutes an estimated 19.6% of the total population of DC as of 2020 (in all age brackets). According to Data from the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Service Administration (SAMHSA,2019), the District of Columbia ranked highest in percentage of adults who used marijuana, followed by the state of Oregon with an estimated 17.9% of adults users of marijuana during the same period.
